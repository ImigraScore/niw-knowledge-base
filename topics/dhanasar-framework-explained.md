# Dhanasar Framework Explained: Adjudication Logic for Professionals

> Translating the Matter of Dhanasar standard (2016) into an operational decision model for EB-2 NIW petitions.

---

## The Origin of the Standard

Since the **Matter of Dhanasar, 26 I&N Dec. 884 (AAO 2016)** decision, U.S. immigration has moved away from the restrictive *NYSDOT* test toward a more flexible, yet highly analytical, three-prong framework.

This page explains the *logic* behind the prongs as applied in the [EB-2 NIW Technical Framework](https://github.com/ImigraScore/eb2-niw-framework).

---

## Prong 1: Substantial Merit & National Importance

### The Adjudication Focus: The *Endeavor*, not the *Person*.
USCIS first asks: Is what you plan to do important?
*   **Substantial Merit:** Does the work have value in the sciences, arts, business, or education? (Usually easy to prove).
*   **National Importance:** Does the impact extend beyond the petitioner's immediate employer? Does it have the potential for broader implications in a field, region, or industry? (This is where most petitions fail).

**Strategic Tip:** Use the [Proposed Endeavor Construction Methodology](https://github.com/ImigraScore/eb2-niw-framework/blob/main/guides/proposed-endeavor.md) to define your impact.

---

## Prong 2: Well Positioned to Advance the Endeavor

### The Adjudication Focus: The *Person*, not the *Endeavor*.
USCIS asks: Are you the right person to lead this work?
*   **Factors:** Education, skills, record of similar successes, progress toward the endeavor (patents, business plans, investors, interest from government agencies).
*   **Common Trap:** Listing achievements that are not related to the *proposed endeavor*.

---

## Prong 3: Balancing the Interests

### The Adjudication Focus: The *Waiver Necessity*.
USCIS asks: Is it beneficial to the U.S. to skip the Labor Certification?
*   **The Logic:** Even if a U.S. worker could do the job, is the applicant's contribution so valuable that requiring a job offer would be impractical or contrary to the national interest?

---

## Why Structure Matters

Adjudicators review thousands of cases. A petition that follows the [Dhanasar Technical Framework](https://github.com/ImigraScore/eb2-niw-framework/blob/main/guides/dhanasar-framework.md) reduces cognitive load for the officer and increases the clarity of the legal argument.
